iPad Stand

I made a combination stand for my iPad Mini and my iPhone 7 that attetches to my desk. The stand is based off of the Multifunction Arm Mounting System by RostaP.

The desk mount isn't the best and required partially dissasembling my desk to install, but now that it's attached it feels very sturdy and I can comfortably mess around with my iPad on the stand without having to worry about breaking anything.


Stl files and more information can be found on Printables.
